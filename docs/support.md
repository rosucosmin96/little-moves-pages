## Common Questions

### How do reminders work?

Little Moves sends gentle reminders during your selected active hours. After you log an activity, the next reminder is scheduled based on your current reminder interval.

### Does Little Moves require an account?

No. Little Moves does not require account creation.

### Where is my activity history stored?

Your activity history and reminder preferences are stored locally on your device.

### Does restoring purchases restore my activity history?

No. Restoring purchases restores access to eligible Pro features tied to your Apple account. It does not restore local activity history on a new device.

If you want to keep your history when moving to a new iPhone, restore the phone from an iPhone or iCloud backup.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. If you have an active Pro subscription, use the in-app subscription management option or your Apple account subscription settings.
