# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Little Moves handles information when you use the app.

## Summary

Little Moves is designed to work without an account and without collecting personal information from you. Your activity logs, reminder preferences, and similar app data are stored locally on your device so the app can function.

## Information We Collect

Little Moves does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as your:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location
- Contacts
- Health data
- Photos
- Usage analytics tied to your identity

## Local Data Storage

Little Moves stores app data locally on your device, including:

- Activity logs you create
- Reminder settings and notification preferences
- App preferences related to Pro access or feature settings

This data is used only to provide the app's features. It is not uploaded to the developer's servers.

Because the app stores data locally, deleting the app or moving to a new device may remove your history unless your device backup restores it.

## Notifications

Little Moves may request permission to send local notifications that remind you to move.

These notifications are scheduled on your device. The app does not need to collect or transmit personal information to send them.

You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings.

## Purchases and Subscriptions

Little Moves may offer optional paid features through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

Payments, billing, renewals, and subscription management are handled by Apple. The developer does not receive or store your full payment information.

If Pro features are enabled, the app may use a service such as RevenueCat to validate subscription status and unlock paid features. That processing is limited to purchase-related status needed for the app to work.

## Third-Party Services

Little Moves may rely on third-party services that support app functionality, including:

- Apple App Store and In-App Purchases
- RevenueCat, if used for subscription status management

These services operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

Little Moves does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Children's Privacy

Little Moves is not directed to children under 13, and the app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date.
